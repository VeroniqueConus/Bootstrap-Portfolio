About Me

Celebrity social media wrangler by day, tired web development student by night! I know what you're thinking, but I assure you, we do exist!

I have worked in the social media space for over 13 years, paying many high-profile people millions of dollars for posting sponsored social media ads that I write! The upside to that is the occasional free product. The downside is ...well, there aren't all that many...but a change of pace, environment and challenging new skillset are always welcomed!

I'm a serial learner. I love to learn. I am especially thrilled to learn any combination of things that pique my interest, such as: history, boardgames, food, The Simpsons, world religions, time travel, fantasy and science fiction, or paranormal activities. Therefore, history of food? LOVE! Boardgames about time travel- awesome! World Religion/Philosophy through the eyes of The Simpsons? That's what I'm talking about!

You think I'd have a lot of time what with my social media job (yes, it's a real job), but actually, I have two mini versions of myself: Genevieve and Juliette. We spend a lot of time at the local library, planning our costumes for our yearly trip to Megacon and trying to recreate dishes from baking shows. Lovely to meet you!
